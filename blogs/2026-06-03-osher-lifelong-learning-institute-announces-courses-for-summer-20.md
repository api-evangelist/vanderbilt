The Osher Lifelong Learning Institute at Vanderbilt is inviting adults aged 50 and older to enroll in diverse summer courses spanning history, literature, arts, science, and wellness. Registration opens June 1-28, with classes beginning July 6, 2026. Members receive benefits including free parking, library privileges, and access to special university programming.
