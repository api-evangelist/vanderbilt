Vanderbilt University has expanded its generative AI offerings to include ChatGPT EDU, Amplify 2.0, and Grow with Google for faculty, students, and staff. Practical implementations across campus include students using AI tools for research, nursing faculty creating virtual patient simulations, and finance staff automating workflows. An AI Day in Practice event is scheduled for September 16 to showcase how the university community is leveraging these technologies.
